About Blackjack

This game is build for fun purposes only. Player will be playing aginst the Dealer.

Player needs to score more then the Dealer to win the game. Player must score under 22 and if the Player score more than 21 then the Player will bust and the Dealer will be the winner. If both the Player & the Dealer score more then 21 and bust then the game will draw.

The game starts by pressing the Hit button. Player will press it multiple times to score the highest under 22 and to start the Dealer's turn the Player will press the Stand button. When the Dealer's turn end the game will show the result. And to start the game agian the Player will the Deal button.
